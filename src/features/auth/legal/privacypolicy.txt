This Privacy Policy explains how TripCast collects, uses, stores, and shares information for private, invite-only trip sharing.

This policy is written as a practical placeholder for private use. It should be reviewed before TripCast is offered more broadly.

1. What TripCast is

TripCast is an invite-only app that lets a Traveler share trip activity with invited Followers.

TripCast may include live location, map pins, Stories, photos, messages, Missions, Route Votes, Travel Funds, Traveler state, achievements, badges, and debug tools.

2. Who should use TripCast

TripCast is not intended for minors. People under 18 should not use TripCast.

If you believe a minor has used TripCast, contact the Traveler who invited them or follow the contact instructions provided with the trip.

3. Information collected

TripCast may collect and store the following types of information:

Account and access information:
- username;
- email address if used;
- password or password-related authentication information;
- display name;
- invite or membership information;
- role, such as Traveler or Follower.

Trip content:
- Stories;
- photos and media;
- messages;
- activity feed events;
- Missions;
- Route Votes;
- comments, reactions, and participation;
- achievements, badges, points, and attribution.

Location information:
- live GPS location from the Traveler if enabled or implemented;
- map pins;
- Story locations;
- Mission locations;
- Route Vote locations;
- Trail Lines or route-related map data.

Traveler state information:
- mood;
- energy;
- fullness or food-related state;
- local time;
- estimated or forecasted state values.

Travel Funds information:
- costs;
- estimated costs;
- transaction notes;
- categories;
- currencies;
- budget-related entries.

Device and app storage:
- localStorage and sessionStorage data used to keep app preferences, local state, debug settings, read/unread state, and similar app behavior.

Debug information:
- local debug logs created in the browser;
- copied or exported debug summaries if a user chooses to share them.

4. Live location

TripCast may collect and store the Traveler’s live GPS location in the backend.

Live location may be shared with invited Followers inside the trip.

Live location can be sensitive. Followers should not misuse it, share it outside the trip, or use it to pressure, stalk, harass, or endanger the Traveler.

Location may be inaccurate, delayed, unavailable, or incomplete.

5. Photos and media

Photos and media added to Stories or other trip content may be uploaded to backend storage.

Photos and media may be visible to authorized trip members.

Users should avoid uploading sensitive images, private documents, identification documents, financial records, or anything they would not want trip members to see.

6. Messages and activity feed

Messages and activity feed items may be stored persistently.

They may be visible to all authorized trip members.

Deleted messages may be removed from the visible app experience where supported, but true deletion of all related backend records, backups, or already-viewed copies may not be available yet.

7. Travel Funds and financial entries

Travel Funds entries may be real financial notes or informal budget notes.

TripCast does not process payments, store payment cards, connect to bank accounts, or complete purchases.

Users should not enter payment card numbers, bank account numbers, Social Security numbers, tax IDs, or other highly sensitive financial information.

8. Debug logs

TripCast debug logs are intended to be local to the user’s browser.

TripCast does not intentionally send debug logs to the backend.

A user may choose to copy, export, or share debug logs. Users should review debug logs before sharing them.

9. Browser storage

TripCast may use localStorage and sessionStorage for app preferences, interface state, read/unread state, debug settings, local cache, and similar features.

TripCast does not use analytics or tracking services beyond the app behavior described in this policy.

10. Third-party services

TripCast may rely on third-party services to operate the app, including:

- Convex for backend data, functions, and storage;
- GitHub Pages for hosting;
- MapLibre for map rendering;
- OpenFreeMap or other map tile providers for map data;
- backend storage services used for photos or media.

These services may process information as needed to provide hosting, backend, storage, and map functionality.

11. How information is used

TripCast uses information to:

- provide access to private trips;
- show trip content to authorized members;
- display live location and map features;
- support Stories, photos, messages, Missions, Route Votes, and Travel Funds;
- show Traveler state and estimated status;
- support achievements, badges, participation, and attribution;
- maintain app preferences and read/unread state;
- diagnose bugs when users choose to use debug tools;
- protect access and prevent misuse.

12. Who can see trip information

Authorized trip members may see content inside the trip.

Travelers may be able to manage, edit, remove, or control trip content and Follower access.

Followers may see Traveler-shared content, messages, trip activity, location information, Stories, photos, Missions, Route Votes, achievements, and other trip information depending on the app’s current features and settings.

If a Follower is removed from a trip, their access is cut off.

13. Sharing outside the trip

TripCast is designed for private trip groups.

Users should not copy, screenshot, repost, export, or share another person’s private trip content outside the trip without permission.

TripCast may still rely on service providers listed above to operate the app.

14. Deletion, hiding, and access requests

Some content may be deleteable or hideable in the app.

Some participation may be obscured where the app supports that behavior.

True deletion is not yet fully self-service.

For removal, access, or deletion requests beyond what the app supports, contact the Traveler who invited you or follow the contact instructions provided with the trip.

Deletion may not remove information from screenshots, exports, copied debug logs, backups, cached files, or content already seen or saved by other trip members.

15. Data retention

TripCast keeps information for as long as needed to operate the trip, provide app features, maintain the trip archive, troubleshoot issues, or support access control.

Specific retention periods have not yet been finalized.

16. Security

TripCast uses app and backend controls intended to limit trip access to authorized users.

No app can guarantee perfect security.

Users should avoid entering highly sensitive information into TripCast and should protect their account credentials and invite links.

17. Children and minors

TripCast is not intended for minors and should not be used by anyone under 18.

TripCast does not knowingly invite or target minors.

If a minor has access, their access should be removed and a request should be made to remove related information where possible.

18. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as TripCast changes.

If the app changes how it collects, uses, stores, or shares information, this policy should be updated to match the app’s actual behavior.

19. Questions or requests

For questions, access requests, removal requests, or privacy concerns, contact the Traveler who invited you or follow the contact instructions provided with the trip.

Do not send highly sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for the request.
